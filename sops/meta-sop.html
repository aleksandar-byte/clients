1. Service pages get target keywords.

Service pages should target one specific service intent.

Negative
Our Dental Services | Lorton Town Dental
Positive
Dental Implants in Lorton, VA | Lorton Town Dental

2. Non-service pages stay descriptive.

About, team, reviews, blog, contact, and policy pages should not compete with service pages.

Negative
Best Dentist in Lorton, VA | Meet Our Dentists
Positive
Meet Our Dentists | Lorton Town Dental

3. Remove generic CTAs.

Use the space to explain the page, not repeat “call today” language.

Negative
Call today to book an appointment and learn more.
Positive
Emergency dentist in Lorton, VA for tooth pain, broken teeth, swelling, and urgent dental concerns.

4. Avoid keyword overlap.

One page should own one clear search intent.

Negative
Homepage: family dentist Lorton
Family page: family dentist Lorton
Positive
Homepage: family dentist Lorton
Family page: family dentistry Lorton VA

5. Use treatment/problem terms.

Koray-style clarity means real entities, symptoms, treatments, and outcomes.

Negative
Exceptional gum treatment with high-quality care.
Positive
Gum disease treatment in Lorton, VA addresses bleeding gums, inflammation, bone loss, and periodontal health.

6. Keep titles clean.

Avoid hype and unnecessary modifiers.

Negative
Best Family Dentist in Lorton, VA
Positive
Family Dentist in Lorton, VA | Lorton Town Dental
