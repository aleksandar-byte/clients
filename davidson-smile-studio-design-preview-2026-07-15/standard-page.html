Patient information

Simple answers for planning an appointment, preparing for your visit, and reaching The Smile Studio in Lake Orion.

On this page

Whether you are looking for routine family care, considering a cosmetic treatment, replacing a missing tooth, or dealing with an urgent concern, the team can help you choose an appropriate first visit.

Planning your visit

Appointments may be requested online, by phone, or by email. Share a brief description of what you would like help with, along with the best way to reach you.

Appointment requests need confirmation.

An online or email request is not a confirmed appointment until you hear back from The Smile Studio team.

When you contact the office

It can help to mention whether you are a new or returning patient and whether your concern feels urgent. You can also ask about a free consultation for services you are considering.

What to bring

Having a few details ready can make check-in and your conversation with the team easier.

  • A current photo ID
  • Your dental insurance information, if applicable
  • A list of medications and relevant health information
  • Questions or concerns you want to discuss

If your plans change

Please contact the office as early as possible if you need to cancel or reschedule. The team can help you find another available time.

Insurance and savings

The Smile Studio accepts most PPO dental insurance plans. Patients without insurance can ask the team about the Dental Savings Plan.

Questions to ask before treatment

  1. Is my dental plan accepted?
  2. What information does the office need from me?
  3. Is the Dental Savings Plan relevant to my visit?
  4. What are the next steps after my consultation or exam?

Insurance information from the office is provided as a courtesy. Coverage and patient responsibility depend on the terms of your individual plan.
