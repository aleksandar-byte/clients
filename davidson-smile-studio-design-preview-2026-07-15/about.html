Our approach

A welcoming dental home in Lake Orion.

The Smile Studio provides preventive, cosmetic, and restorative dental services in a warm, welcoming environment. The team helps patients understand their options and build a plan around their individual needs.

From routine cleanings and children’s dentistry to braces, Invisalign, veneers, implants, and smile makeovers, care is coordinated in one practice with comfort at the center of each visit.
