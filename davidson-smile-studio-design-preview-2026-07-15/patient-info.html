Insurance

Bring your questions. We will help you sort through the details.

The Smile Studio accepts most PPO dental insurances. Because every plan is different, call the office with your plan information before your visit so the team can help you understand the next steps.

Using PPO insurance

Start with your plan details.

Have your insurance card available when you call and bring it with you to your appointment. The team can help with insurance questions, while you remain responsible for charges your plan does not cover.

No insurance?

Ask about the Dental Savings Plan.

The Dental Savings Plan gives patients without dental insurance another path to plan for care. An insurance specialist can help you find an option that meets your dental needs and budget.

Financing and payment options

Know the financial next step before treatment begins.

Payment is expected when services are provided unless other arrangements have been made in advance. Before treatment, ask the team about approved payment methods and whether financing or another arrangement may be available for your care.

Ask for an estimate based on your recommended care. Review what your insurance may cover. Ask about financing or payment arrangements before your appointment.

Your first visit

A few simple details help your visit run smoothly.

If you are new to The Smile Studio, arrive with the information the team needs to understand your health, contact preferences, and insurance coverage.

  1. 01

    Request your appointment

    Call or send a request online. Your visit is confirmed once you hear back from the office.

  2. 02

    Bring current information

    Have your photo ID, insurance card if applicable, and an accurate health and medication history.

  3. 03

    Share your priorities

    Tell the team what brought you in, what you hope to change, and any questions you want answered.

Common questions

Helpful answers before you call.

Do you accept dental insurance?

The Smile Studio accepts most PPO dental insurances. Call with your plan information for help understanding the next step.

What if I do not have insurance?

Ask about the Dental Savings Plan. The office's insurance specialist can help you explore an option that meets your needs and budget.

Is my online request a confirmed appointment?

No. An appointment request is confirmed after you receive a response from the office team.

What should I do if I need to reschedule?

Call the office as early as possible so the team can help you find another time.
