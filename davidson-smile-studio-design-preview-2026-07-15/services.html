What happens next

Your visit starts with listening, not guessing.

Some concerns have more than one reasonable answer. Your dentist will first learn what is bothering you, examine your teeth and bite, and explain the options that fit what they find.

  1. 01
    Share your concern

    Tell the team about symptoms, goals, timing, and anything that has made care difficult before.

  2. 02
    Understand what is happening

    An exam and any appropriate imaging help your dentist see the full picture.

  3. 03
    Compare the paths

    Review benefits, limitations, timing, and costs before deciding what feels right.

Common questions

Before you choose a service

What if I do not know what kind of appointment I need?

That is completely fine. Describe what you are noticing when you contact the office, and the team can help you choose an appropriate starting visit.

Can my family receive different types of care in the same office?

The Smile Studio provides family dentistry, cleanings, orthodontic care, cosmetic dentistry, implant care, TMJ relief, and dental sleep medicine in Lake Orion.

Do you accept dental insurance?

The practice accepts PPO insurance and also offers a Dental Savings Plan. Coverage varies, so the team can help you understand the information available for your plan.

Can I ask about more than one concern?

Yes. Bring up every concern that matters to you. Your dentist can help prioritize oral health needs and discuss how different treatments may fit together.
