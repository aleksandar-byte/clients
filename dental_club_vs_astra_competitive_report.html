7. CRO Weaknesses on The Dental Club

Weak Points

  • Weak hero clarity and value proposition
  • Limited trust-building elements above the fold
  • CTA hierarchy is likely not strong or prominent enough
  • Less persuasive service-page structure
  • Weaker local proof and authority signals
  • Less depth in treatment explanation and benefits
  • Fewer conversion-supporting elements such as reviews, financing, reassurance, FAQs, and process clarity
  • Likely weaker internal paths from informational intent to booking intent

What The Dental Club Needs to Do

  • Rewrite the hero so the offer is instantly clear: who they help, what they do, and why choose them
  • Strengthen primary CTA visibility with a clear booking or request appointment action
  • Add trust signals near the top of the page: reviews, years of experience, doctor credibility, insurance or financing, and key benefits
  • Make service pages more conversion-focused with clearer structure, treatment benefits, candidacy, process, outcomes, and FAQs
  • Improve local reassurance by emphasizing Stratford relevance, patient trust, and convenience
  • Add stronger proof throughout the site: testimonials, before-and-after examples, technology, team, and patient experience details
  • Reduce friction by improving mobile CTA placement, contact visibility, and appointment pathways
  • Build better internal linking from blog and support pages into core money pages
  • Create a clearer section flow so users move naturally from problem to solution to trust to CTA
